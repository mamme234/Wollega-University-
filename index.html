🏛 Our History

Founded in 2007, Wollega University has grown into one of Ethiopia's premier institutions of higher learning. With a commitment to academic excellence and community development, we have educated thousands of leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

Mission

To provide quality education, conduct impactful research, and serve our community.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Africa by 2030.

Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, Excellence
